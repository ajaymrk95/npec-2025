Sponsorship for 12th National Power Electronics Conference (NPEC 2025)

About Conference

The National Power Electronics Conference (NPEC) is a biennial conference of Power Electronics forum in India. The conference focuses on dissemination of the potential research findings in Industries and Academia. The NPEC was first organized at IIT Bombay in 2003 and subsequently in IIT Kharagpur (2005), IISc Bangalore (2007), IIT Roorkee (2010), IIEST Shipur (2011), IIT Kanpur (2013), IIT Bombay (2015), College of Engineering, Pune (2017), National Institute of Technology, Tiruchirappalli (2019), and IIT Bhubaneswar (2021), IIT Guwahati (2023) to enhance the interaction of the peers in the field of Power Electronics.
