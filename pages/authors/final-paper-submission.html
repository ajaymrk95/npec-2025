Final Paper Submission

Submission Guidelines

  • Submit the final paper in IEEE two-column format as a .pdf file not exceeding six (6) A4 pages (max size 2MB).
    Download the template: IEEE - Manuscript Templates
  • Authors must ensure plagiarism check before uploading to IEEE Xplore. More details here.
  • Paper title and author order must remain unchanged in final submission.
  • Accepted papers will be published in IEEE Xplore only if at least one author registers (full registration) and presents at the conference.
  • Reviewers' comments must be addressed in the final paper.

Submission of the Copyright Form to IEEE

  • Review paper details before submitting the copyright form.
  • After passing the PDF check, click 'Submit IEEE copyright form' in the Author Console.
  • Read instructions on the IEEE Copyright Form Submission page before proceeding.
  • Submit the form on the IEEE Copyright Web Site and download a copy.
  • Upload the completed form in the Author Console by dragging or selecting 'Upload from Computer' then click 'Save'.
