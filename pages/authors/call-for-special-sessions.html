Proposal Guidelines

The NPEC 2025 invites special sessions (SSs) proposal on emerging topics which are not listed in conference tracks. A proposal for SS should include following information

  • Title of the Special Session
  • Brief description (approx. 100 words) highlighting the importance of the topic
  • Names and contact details of 2-3 session chairs
  • Short IEEE-style CV of the organizers
  • List of at least 10 potential authors and their affiliations

Organization of Special Sessions

  • Publicity: SS organizers should promote their session to ensure sufficient paper submissions.
  • Review Process: Similar to regular papers, reviews must be done by SS organizers, with final decisions made by the session co-chairs.
  • Conflict of Interest: SS organizers must declare conflicts of interest before reviewing papers.
  • Restrictions:
    • An individual cannot be (co-)author of more than two papers in the same session.
    • Papers from organizers must not exceed 60% of the session content.
  • Successful Special Session Criteria: At least 6 accepted papers from at least 3 independent institutions.
  • Waiver: A complimentary full registration is granted for successful sessions with at least 6 papers.
