Call for Student Poster Presentation

To share research work and creative ideas with other attendees, we invite students to participate in the Student Poster Presentation (SPP) at NPEC 2025. This is an excellent opportunity to receive feedback, network with like-minded professionals, and showcase research innovations. The event will feature keynote sessions, tutorials, and regular paper presentations.

Important Points for SPP Submission

  • Submit a PowerPoint presentation of unpublished or published research work for review.
  • Accepted posters will not be included in the conference proceedings.
  • A limited number of posters will be accepted for presentation.
  • Student registration is mandatory to present a poster at the SPP event.
  • Registration includes access to all conference activities, meals (breakfast, lunch, and dinner), and coffee breaks.
  • Depending on availability, student participants may receive FREE hostel accommodation for four days (13-12-2025 to 17-12-2025) in double occupancy rooms.

Important Dates

  • Last date to submit Poster: 25.11.2025
  • Notification of Acceptance: 27.11.2025
  • Post-Acceptance Requirement: Accepted students must register for the conference and present their poster at NPEC 2025.
