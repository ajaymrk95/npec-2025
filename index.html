About the Conference

The National Power Electronics Conference (NPEC 2025) is a biennial conference of Power Electronics forum in India. The conference focuses on dissemination of the potential research findings in Industries and Academia. The NPEC was first organized at IIT Bombay in 2003 and subsequently in IIT Kharagpur (2005), IISc Bangalore (2007), IIT Roorkee (2010), IIEST Shipur (2011), IIT Kanpur (2013), IIT Bombay (2015), College of Engineering, Pune (2017), National Institute of Technology Tiruchirappalli (2019), IIT Bhubaneswar (2021) and Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati (2023) to enhance the interaction of the peers in the field of Power Electronics.

The 11th edition of this prestigious conference (NPEC 2025) is to be held at National Institute of Technology Calicut during 14-16 December 2025. It would be a proud moment for NIT Calicut to organize this conference which will be a formal get-together for the power electronics community. It features extensive exposition on Power Electronics through (1) Plenary talks, (2) Panel discussions, (3) Tutorials and (4) Paper Presentations.

This NPEC 2025 will be a launch pad for many researchers to exhibit the influence of Power Electronics on various applications viz. industry, domestic, commercial, mobility and electric utility systems. Technical sessions by industrial experts will bring out the recent challenges faced in industry which will provide an opportunity for inspirational minds to solve them. This will lead to a sturdy power electronic community comprising of academicians, research scholars and industry professionals. The development of new circuits and tools for the power electronic systems will also be an outcome of this conference.
